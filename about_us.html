    Create a seller profile by registering with your email or phone number. Provide your store name, logo, and a brief business description to start listing your products.
  • Define your delivery areas.
    Specify the locations where you're willing to deliver--whether it's a local pin code, city, district, state, or pan-India. This helps in targeting the right customers.
  • Flexible pricing options.
    Set unique prices for different regions or states depending on logistics, demand, or local competition. Maintain profitability with smart pricing tools.
  • Product management tools.
    Easily add, update, or remove product listings. Monitor inventory levels and manage product availability in real-time to avoid overselling.

    You can also list used or refurbished products. Just pay a small listing fee and provide clear descriptions and images for transparency.
  • Buyer-seller messaging.
    An in-app chat system is enabled for used products, allowing buyers to ask questions and negotiate before making a purchase.

    Our system deducts a small platform commission on successful sales. Remaining amounts are securely transferred to the seller's registered bank account.

    Once an item is marked delivered by the seller, buyers are prompted to confirm delivery and condition. This step protects both parties and ensures transparency.
  • Seller ratings and reputation.

For Buyers

  • Browse for your product.
    Navigate effortlessly through intuitive categories, filters, and search options to find exactly what you're looking for. Explore trending deals and curated collections.
  • Smart product recommendations.
    Based on your browsing and purchase history, our system suggests relevant and similar products to improve your shopping experience.
  • Detailed product information.
    Each product page provides rich details including specifications, seller info, warranty (if applicable), shipping options, and customer reviews.
  • Ratings and reviews.
    After purchasing a product, rate it and share your feedback through text, images, or even video reviews. Help others make informed choices.
  • Purchase protection.
    Your payments are held securely until the product is delivered and confirmed in good condition. If there's an issue, raise a dispute directly through the app.
  • In-app communication.
    For used products, initiate a conversation with the seller via chat to ask questions, request more images, or negotiate the price.
  • Wishlist and alerts.
    Add products to your wishlist and receive price drop alerts or back-in-stock notifications for better shopping convenience.
